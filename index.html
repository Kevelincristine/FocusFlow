FocusFlow

FocusFlow is a simple and effective productivity app designed to help you organize your tasks and stay focused. Start by adding your tasks to the to-do list. Choose one task, start the Pomodoro timer, and work with full focus for 25 minutes. After each focus session, take a short break and then continue with the next task. This method helps reduce distractions, improve concentration, and make your work more manageable. FocusFlow is ideal for studying, working, or planning your day in a calm and organized way.
